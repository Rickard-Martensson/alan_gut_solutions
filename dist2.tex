$\begin{array}{l}

\end{array}$ & 
$\begin{array}{r}

\displaystyle \varphi_X(t) = e^{it\mu - \frac{1}{2} \sigma^2 t^2}
\end{array}$ \\
\multicolumn{4}{p{19cm}}{The Normal distribution is central to the central limit theorem} \\

    \Gamma(p, a) \\

\end{array}$ &
